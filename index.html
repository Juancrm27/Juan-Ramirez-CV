Hello! I'm Juan Ramírez, a Graphic Designer and 3D Artist born in Quito and currently based in Buenos Aires.

These days, I work as a Visual Designer and 2D/3D Asset Creator at 3Dar, a technology company that creates immersive AR/VR experiences for projects in META. Previously, I was part of the team at Camon, a tech startup focused on AR, where I contributed as a Visual and Interaction Designer to the development of innovative solutions for clients worldwide.

21Studio is my personal project where I create interactive experiences and explore new technologies. My goal is to merge creativity with innovation, providing users with unique and immersive experiences. Furthermore, in 2023, I launched jcrm©, my own brand of clothing and accessories.

In addition to my main job, I'm passionate about side projects where I aim to blend my skills in 3D with Blender.
